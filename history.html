A Legacy Like No Other

More than a century of greatness.

1902: The Birth of Real Madrid

A small group of football lovers founded Madrid Football Club, marking the start of one of the greatest dynasties in sport.

1950–1960: The Di Stéfano Era

Real Madrid dominated Europe, winning the first five European Cups and establishing global superiority.

1980s–1990s: Modernization

A new generation of stars and infrastructure pushed the club into a modern football powerhouse.

2000–2006: Galácticos Era

Florentino Pérez introduced the superstar era, signing the biggest football icons and elevating the brand worldwide.

2014–2018: The Three-peat

Under Zidane, Real Madrid achieved history by winning three consecutive Champions League titles — a record unmatched in the modern era.
